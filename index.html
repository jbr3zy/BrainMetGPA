The Graded Prognostic Assessment (GPA) mobile app is a tool that helps provide a diagnosis-specific estimate of survival for patients with brain metastases.
Read more...

The Graded Prognostic Assessment (GPA) estimates survival for patients with brain metastases. Actual outcomes for individual patients can vary widely.

Survival and the factors that predict survival vary by diagnosis. Patient care should be individualized based on the patient's prognosis.

These indices have been developed based on the experience of 11 institutions with 3940 patients (Sperduto et al, Journal of Clinical Oncology 2012:30;419-425).

The GPA has been independently validated by numerous publications from around the world and is now used to stratify clinical trials.
